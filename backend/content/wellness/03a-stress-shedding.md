# Day 3A — Stress Shedding

**Tier: Starter | Time: 8 min | Week: Foundation**

## The Why
Stress isn't just mental — it lives in your body. Tight shoulders, clenched jaw, shallow breathing, restless sleep. This routine is about consciously releasing the physical grip of stress.

## The Routine

### 1. Body Scan (2 min)
- Close your eyes. Sit comfortably.
- Starting at your toes, mentally scan up through your body
- When you find tension, pause and breathe into that spot
- Areas to check: jaw, shoulders, hands, stomach, hips

### 2. Tension-Release (2 min)
- Tense your shoulders up to your ears as hard as you can — hold 5s
- Release completely. Feel the drop. Breathe.
- Repeat 3 times
- Then do the same with fists (squeeze 5s, release)
- Then jaw (clench 5s, release, let lips part)

### 3. Box Breathing (3 min)
- Inhale 4 counts
- Hold 4 counts
- Exhale 4 counts
- Hold 4 counts (empty)
- Repeat 6-8 cycles

### 4. Gratitude Micro-Shift (1 min)
- Think of ONE thing that went right today
- Say it out loud or write it down
- Let that sit before you move on

## Builder Add-on (+5 min)
- 5-minute guided visualization: imagine your stress as a color or shape. Watch it dissolve with each exhale.
- Journal 3 things you're letting go of today

## Thriver Add-on (+10 min)
- Complete the Tension-Release sequence twice (morning + evening)
- Schedule one "worry block" (15 min to write down everything worrying you, then close the notebook)

## Tracking Prompt
> "Where did I feel stress in my body today? Did the release exercise change anything?"

## Habit Anchor
Set a phone timer for 3x daily (10am, 2pm, 6pm) — when it rings, take 3 conscious breaths before continuing.
