# Day 5B — Movement Snacks

**Tier: All levels | Time: 2-5 min each | Week: Build**

## The Why
Sitting is the new smoking. Every hour you sit without breaking it up, your metabolic rate drops, blood pools in your legs, and your brain fog thickens. "Movement snacks" are 1-5 minute bursts of activity sprinkled through your day.

## The Menu (pick 2-3 today)

### Snack 1: The Stair Climb (2 min)
- Find a staircase
- Walk up and down for 2 min
- Bonus: take two stairs at a time

### Snack 2: The Desk Reset (3 min)
- Stand up, push chair in
- 10 calf raises (slow, full range)
- 10 desk push-ups (hands on desk edge, lean in, push back)
- 10 seated leg extensions (straighten one leg, hold 5s, repeat)
- 10 shoulder rolls (5 each direction)

### Snack 3: The Parking Lot Lap (5 min)
- Walk at brisk pace around the building or parking lot
- Bonus: find a curb and do 10 heel drops (heels hang off curb, drop down, lift up)

### Snack 4: The Bathroom Bounce (1 min)
- After using the restroom, do 20 jumping jacks or 10 deep squats
- Nobody's watching — and if they are, they'll probably join next week

### Snack 5: The Afternoon Reset (5 min)
- Stand at a window or go outside
- Look at something far away (20+ feet) for 30s — resets eye strain
- 10 deep breaths
- 5 torso twists each side
- 5 side stretches each side

## Game Plan
**Today's Action:** Set 3 phone alarms: 10am, 2pm, 4pm. Each alarm = one movement snack. Pick from the menu.

## Starter Goal
At least ONE movement snack today. That's a win.

## Thriver Goal
Every hour on the hour, stand up and move for 1-2 min. Use a standing desk if available.

## Tracking Prompt
> "How many hours did I sit without getting up today? What was my longest sitting streak?"

## Habit Anchor
Any time you have a bathroom break = movement snack trigger. Any time you finish a meeting/phone call = stand up and stretch for 30s.
