# Day 3B — Breathwork & Recovery

**Tier: Builder | Time: 12 min | Week: Build**

## The Why
Most mid-lifers breathe shallowly (chest breathing), keeping their nervous system in a low-grade fight-or-flight state. Deep, intentional breathing shifts you into parasympathetic (rest & digest) mode. It's the cheapest biohack available.

## The Practice

### 1. 4-7-8 Breathing (5 min)
- Sit comfortably, back straight
- Inhale through nose for 4 counts
- Hold breath for 7 counts
- Exhale through mouth for 8 counts (pursed lips)
- Repeat 6-8 times

**Why:** The extended exhale activates the vagus nerve, lowering heart rate and cortisol.

### 2. Diaphragmatic Breathing (4 min)
- Lie on your back, one hand on chest, one on belly
- Breathe so only the belly hand rises — chest stays still
- Inhale slow for 4, exhale for 6
- 10 breaths

### 3. Alternate Nostril Breathing (3 min)
- Close right nostril with thumb, inhale through left (4 counts)
- Close left nostril with ring finger, hold (4 counts)
- Release right nostril, exhale (6 counts)
- Inhale through right (4 counts), hold (4), exhale through left (6)
- 5 full cycles

## Starter Variation
- Just the 4-7-8 breathing, 4 cycles — done in 3 min
- Do it sitting in your car before going into the house after work

## Thriver Variation
- Add a cold shower after breathwork (30s cold, 90s warm, repeat 3x)
- Do breathwork before AND after a stressful event (meeting, presentation)
- Combine with the stress shedding routine from Day 3A

## When to Use Each Technique
| Situation | Technique |
|-----------|-----------|
| Can't sleep | 4-7-8 breathing |
| Anxious before a meeting | Box breathing (4-4-4-4) |
| Racing thoughts | Diaphragmatic (belly breathing) |
| Need to focus | Alternate nostril |
| Overwhelmed | Long exhale breathing |

## Tracking Prompt
> "How did my mental state shift from before to after the breathwork? Any physical sensations?"

## Habit Anchor
Do 3 conscious breaths before you open any new screen session (phone unlock, laptop wake). This creates hundreds of micro-resets per day.
