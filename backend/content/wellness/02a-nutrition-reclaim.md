# Day 2A — Nutrition Reclaim

**Tier: Starter | Time: 10 min (reading + planning) | Week: Foundation**

## The Why
Mid-life metabolism shifts. You can't eat at 40 like you did at 25 without consequences. This isn't about dieting — it's about stopping the slow bleed of energy and brain fog.

## The Lesson

### The Protein Rule
Most mid-lifers are protein-deficient. You need roughly **0.7g per pound of body weight** (e.g., 140g if you weigh 200lbs).

**Today's Action:** Add one serving of protein to your breakfast. Eggs, Greek yogurt, or a quality protein shake. That's it.

### The Sugar Trap
By 10am, 3pm, and 8pm, your brain will ask for sugar. This is the habit, not true hunger.

**Today's Action:** Create a 3-day sugar pause. Replace one afternoon sugar hit with:
- Sparkling water + lemon
- A handful of almonds
- Herbal tea (peppermint or ginger)

### The Hydration Gap
Fatigue is often dehydration in disguise.

**Today's Action:** Fill a 1L bottle. Finish it before 2pm. Refill. Finish that by bedtime.

## Builder Add-on
- Log everything you eat today in a simple note. No judgment — just data.
- Identify one meal where you can swap refined carbs (white bread/pasta) for whole food alternatives.

## Thriver Add-on
- Calculate your protein target using the formula above
- Plan three meals tomorrow that hit that target
- Prep one meal tonight (overnight oats, chopped veggies, pre-cooked chicken)

## Tracking Prompt
> "How many hours did I go today without feeling an energy crash? What helped?"

## Habit Anchor
Put the 1L water bottle on your desk or kitchen counter tonight. You can't drink what you can't see.
