# Day 1A — Morning Mobility Reset

**Tier: Starter | Time: 8 min | Week: Foundation**

## The Why
After 35, your body stiffens from sitting, staring at screens, and accumulated stress. This daily reset prevents the "can't touch my toes" slide and wakes up your nervous system.

## The Routine

### 1. Cat-Cow (1 min)
- On hands and knees, alternate arching and rounding your spine
- Breathe: inhale as you drop the belly, exhale as you round
- 5 slow reps

### 2. Neck Release (1 min)
- Sit cross-legged or in a chair
- Drop right ear to right shoulder, hold 30s — repeat left
- Then chin to chest, hold 30s

### 3. Hip Circles (2 min)
- Stand feet shoulder-width apart, hands on hips
- Slowly rotate hips clockwise 30s, then counter-clockwise 30s
- Deepen the circle each rotation

### 4. Standing Forward Fold (2 min)
- Fold at hips, let arms dangle
- Bend knees generously if hamstrings are tight
- Hold 90s, then roll up one vertebra at a time

### 5. Side Stretch (1 min)
- Reach right arm overhead, lean left — hold 30s
- Repeat opposite side

### 6. Deep Breaths (1 min)
- Stand tall, hands on ribs
- Inhale for 4, hold 2, exhale for 6
- Repeat 5 cycles

## Builder Add-on (+5 min)
- Add 10 bodyweight squats after hip circles
- Add 30s plank after forward fold

## Thriver Add-on (+10 min)
- Full sun salutation flow (5 rounds)
- 3 rounds of 30s single-leg balance each side

## Tracking Prompt
> "On a scale of 1-10, how did your body feel BEFORE today's mobility? How about AFTER?"

## Habit Anchor
Do this right after your first glass of water in the morning. Stack it on an existing habit.
