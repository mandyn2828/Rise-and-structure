# Day 6B — Outdoor Adventure

**Tier: All levels | Time: 30-60 min | Week: Build**

## The Why
Nature is a performance-enhancing drug with zero side effects. Time outdoors lowers cortisol, boosts mood, improves focus, and increases Vitamin D. The best part: it costs nothing and works every time.

## The Challenge

### Choose Your Adventure

| Activity | Difficulty | Time | Location |
|----------|-----------|------|----------|
| Nature walk in a park | Starter | 30 min | Any park |
| Hike on a local trail | Builder | 45-60 min | Trail nearby |
| Bike ride | Builder | 30-45 min | Bike path |
| Kayak/paddleboard | Thriver | 60 min | Lake/river |
| Trail run | Thriver | 30-45 min | Trail nearby |
| Urban walk (new neighborhood) | Starter | 30 min | Unfamiliar area |

### Today's Action
Pick ONE of the above. Go. No excuses. Weather excuses? Walk in the rain with good gear (it's fun).

### Mindset Rules for Today
1. **No headphones** — listen to the environment for at least the first 15 min
2. **No phone** — unless you're taking a photo of something beautiful
3. **No rush** — this isn't a workout, it's an experience
4. **Notice 5 things** — before you turn around, notice 5 things you wouldn't have seen in the first 5 minutes

## Starter Variation
- Walk around your block with the explicit goal of finding 3 things you've never noticed before
- Sit on a bench in a park for 10 min with no phone

## Thriver Variation
- Combine with a movement snack challenge: do 10 squats at every trail marker
- Bring a notebook and write for 5 min at the turnaround point
- Go with a friend or family member — conversation in nature is different

## Tracking Prompt
> "How did I feel before going outside? After? What changed in my mental state?"

## Habit Anchor
Keep a set of "adventure-ready" gear by the door: comfortable walking shoes, a weather-appropriate layer, a water bottle, and a hat. If it takes 30 seconds to prepare, you'll go.
