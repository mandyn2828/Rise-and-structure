# Day 2B — Meal Prep Systems

**Tier: Builder | Time: 20 min planning | Week: Build**

## The Why
Willpower is a limited resource. If you have to decide what to eat 3x a day, you'll eventually make bad choices when you're tired, stressed, or hungry. Meal prep removes the decision — and the decision is where most diets fail.

## The System

### Step 1: Pick Your Protein (for the week)
Choose 2-3 proteins and prep them in bulk:
- Grilled chicken breast (cook 4-6 at once)
- Hard-boiled eggs (make 8-12)
- Canned wild salmon or tuna
- Lentils or chickpeas (if plant-based)
- Greek yogurt (no prep needed)

### Step 2: Pick Your Veggies (for the week)
Choose 2-3 veggies, wash and chop:
- Broccoli, bell peppers, spinach, zucchini
- Store in glass containers with paper towel (stays fresh longer)

### Step 3: Pick Your Complex Carbs
Choose 1-2:
- Quinoa (cook once, use all week)
- Sweet potatoes (roast 3-4 at once)
- Brown rice or oats

### Step 4: The Assembly Formula
Each meal = Protein (palm size) + Veggies (2 fist sizes) + Carbs (1 cupped hand) + Healthy fat (thumb size)

### Today's Action
Open your calendar. Schedule 1 hour this weekend for "Prep Hour." That's it. Don't do the prep today — just schedule it.

## Starter Variation
- Just prep breakfast: overnight oats in 3 jars takes 10 min
- Buy pre-chopped veggies from the grocery store
- Batch-cook eggs (boiled or scrambled) for 3 days

## Thriver Variation
- Plan macros for the week using a simple app or notebook
- Cost-optimize: where can you buy in bulk? Which meals overlap ingredients?
- Prep lunches and dinners for Mon-Fri in 2 hours on Sunday

## Tracking Prompt
> "Did I eat more consistently today because I had prepped food available?"

## Habit Anchor
Sunday at 4pm = Prep Hour. Set the recurring reminder now. Future you will thank past you every single day next week.
