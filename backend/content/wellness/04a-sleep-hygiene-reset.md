# Day 4A — Sleep Hygiene Reset

**Tier: Starter | Time: 10 min | Week: Foundation**

## The Why
Sleep is the #1 performance enhancer for your 40s. Poor sleep accelerates aging, weight gain, brain fog, and emotional volatility. You cannot out-hustle bad sleep.

## The Prescription

### 1. Wind-Down Window (30 min before bed)
Tonight, 30 minutes before bedtime, do this:
- No screens (phone, laptop, TV)
- Dim the lights
- Set room temp to 65-68°F (18-20°C)
- No work talk, no stressful conversations

**Today's Action:** Pick a bedtime and set a "wind-down" alarm 30 min before.

### 2. The 3-2-1 Rule
- **3 hours before bed** — no more large meals
- **2 hours before bed** — no more work
- **1 hour before bed** — no more screens

**Today's Action:** Write down your last meal time, last work time, last screen time tonight.

### 3. Temperature Drop
Your body needs to cool down to sleep deeply.
- Take a warm shower 60-90 min before bed (the temperature drop after triggers sleep)
- Or keep your bedroom cool (65-68°F / 18-20°C)
- Sleep with socks on if feet get cold

### 4. The Pre-Sleep Ritual (5 min)
Choose ONE tonight:
- Read 2 pages of a physical book
- Write down 3 things you accomplished today
- Listen to one slow song with no lyrics
- 5 minutes of box breathing (from Day 3A)

## Builder Add-on
- Move phone charger out of the bedroom tonight
- Use a physical alarm clock instead of phone alarm
- Blackout the room (even just a sleep mask)

## Thriver Add-on
- Track your sleep using a simple journal (bedtime, wake time, quality 1-10)
- Identify and eliminate one caffeine source after 2pm
- Test sleeping with blue-light blocking glasses for the last hour before bed

## Tracking Prompt
> "How many hours did I actually sleep? Quality 1-10? What helped or hurt?"

## Habit Anchor
Put your phone on the other side of the room before your wind-down starts. The friction of getting up to check it will kill the habit.
