# Wellness Routines Overview

## Philosophy
Small, consistent actions beat heroic bursts. Each routine is designed for a burnt-out mid-lifer with limited time and energy. The goal is **re-engagement with their body** — not perfection.

## Weekly Structure

| Day | Week A (Foundation) | Week B (Build) |
|-----|-------------------|----------------|
| Mon | Morning Mobility Reset | Strength Foundations |
| Tue | Nutrition Reclaim | Meal Prep Systems |
| Wed | Stress Shedding | Breathwork & Recovery |
| Thu | Sleep Hygiene Reset | Sleep Optimization |
| Fri | Energy Audit | Movement Snacks |
| Sat | Active Recovery Walk | Outdoor Adventure |
| Sun | Weekly Review & Plan | Intentional Rest |

## Difficulty Levels
- **Starter**: 5-10 min — just get moving
- **Builder**: 10-15 min — build consistency
- **Thriver**: 15-20 min — deepen the practice

## User Data Needed per Routine
- Age (targeting 35-55 range adjustments)
- Current fitness level (1-5)
- Available time slot (morning/lunch/evening)
- Physical limitations (back, knees, shoulders, etc.)
- Previous day's completion status (for streak motivation)
