# Day 7A — Weekly Review & Plan

**Tier: Starter | Time: 15 min | Week: Foundation**

## The Why
What gets reviewed gets improved. A weekly review closes the loop on your efforts, celebrates wins, and sets you up for a stronger week. This is the habit that multiplies all other habits.

## The Review

### 1. Celebrate (3 min)
Write down 3 wins from this week — no matter how small.
- Did the mobility routine?
- Drank water before coffee?
- Went for a walk?
- Said no to something that drained you?
- *Every win counts.*

### 2. Learn (5 min)
Answer honestly:
- What was the hardest habit to stick with?
- What day was my lowest energy? Why?
- What day was my highest energy? Why?
- I felt proud when I __________.
- I felt guilty when I __________.

### 3. Look Ahead (5 min)
For next week, pick ONE focus:
- **Option A:** Same as this week — double down on consistency
- **Option B:** Level up — add the Builder extension to one routine
- **Option C:** Swap — replace the habit that didn't stick with something new

### 4. Set Your Intention (2 min)
Complete this sentence:
> "Next week, I will __________ because I deserve __________."

## Builder Add-on
- Rate each of the 7 days on wellness effort (1-10) on a simple calendar
- Identify one environmental change (rearrange kitchen, prep workout clothes, etc.)

## Thriver Add-on
- Share your review with an accountability partner
- Schedule next week's "non-negotiable" wellness times into your calendar NOW
- Pre-select one meal-prep recipe for the coming week

## Tracking Prompt
> "On a scale of 1-10, how connected do I feel to my body right now? What would make that number go up by 1 next week?"

## Habit Anchor
Set a recurring Sunday or Monday 15-min calendar block: "Weekly Wellness Review." Protect it like a meeting with your CEO (you are the CEO of your body).
