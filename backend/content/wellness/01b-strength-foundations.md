# Day 1B — Strength Foundations

**Tier: Builder | Time: 15 min | Week: Build**

## The Why
After 35, you naturally lose 3-8% of muscle mass per decade (sarcopenia). Strength training isn't about looking good — it's about bone density, metabolic rate, joint health, and staying independent. This is the minimum effective dose.

## The Routine (Circuit — do 3 rounds)

### 1. Bodyweight Squats (1 min)
- Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out
- Sit back like you're reaching for a chair
- Go as deep as comfortable (parallel is great)
- 10-15 reps (slow and controlled)

### 2. Wall Push-ups (1 min)
- Stand arm's length from a wall
- Lean in, push off
- Keep body straight (don't bend at hips)
- 12-15 reps

### 3. Glute Bridge (1 min)
- Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat
- Squeeze glutes, lift hips to ceiling
- Hold 2s at top, lower slowly
- 10-12 reps

### 4. Plank Hold (30-60s)
- Forearms on ground, body in straight line
- Engage core, don't let hips sag
- Breathe steadily — 30s is a win

### Rest 60s between rounds

## Starter Variation
- 2 rounds instead of 3
- 8-10 reps per exercise
- Knee push-ups instead of wall push-ups (or stay with wall)

## Thriver Add-on
- Add 5-10lb dumbbells for squats (goblet squat)
- Add 10 reps of band rows (attach band to door frame)
- Add 3 rounds of farmer's carry (30s each hand with a heavy object)

## Form Cues
- **Squats:** Weight in heels, knees tracking over toes (not caving in)
- **Push-ups:** Hands under shoulders, core tight, neck neutral
- **Glute bridge:** Don't overextend lower back — squeeze glutes to lift
- **Plank:** Squeeze quads and glutes — this takes pressure off lower back

## Tracking Prompt
> "How did my joints feel during this? Any pain (stop) or just muscular fatigue (good)?"

## Habit Anchor
Do this before your shower on strength days. After 3 weeks, you'll feel wrong skipping it.
