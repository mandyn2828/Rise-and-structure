## Feature Descriptions — The Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Wellness — Daily Movement & Recovery

**Headline:** Your body has a second act. This is how you start it.

**Body:**
Most wellness apps assume you're already fit. We don't.

Every day, you get a 5-15 minute routine designed for bodies over 35 — mobility, strength, nutrition habits, sleep optimization, and stress release. Two weeks of rotating content so you never get bored. Three difficulty tiers (Starter / Builder / Thriver) so you always have a next step.

**What you'll actually do:**
- Morning mobility resets that undo hours of sitting
- Strength foundations that rebuild muscle and bone density
- Nutrition and sleep systems that restore energy
- Stress-shedding practices that work in under 5 minutes

**The promise:** You don't need willpower. You need a routine you can follow. We deliver it daily.

---

### Pillar 2: Mindset — Daily Coaching & Reflection

**Headline:** The voice in your head isn't the truth. It's a habit. Let's change it.

**Body:**
Mid-life is a second adolescence emotionally. Regret, comparison, "is this it?" — these thoughts surface for almost everyone 35-55. You're not broken. You're processing a transition.

Every day, a mindset prompt, reflection questions, and a 3-minute practice — delivered to you at your chosen time. No toxic positivity. No "just manifest it." Real practices built on cognitive science and coaching frameworks.

**Weekly themes rotate:**
- Identity Shift (Mon) — Who am I becoming?
- Self-Compassion (Tue) — Quieting the inner critic
- Purpose Reconnect (Wed) — What matters now
- Emotional Awareness (Thu) — Name it to tame it
- Resilience (Fri) — Reframing struggle
- Connection (Sat) — Relationship with self & others
- Vision (Sun) — Looking forward with intention

---

### Pillar 3: Finance — Practical Money Skills

**Headline:** Get paid what you're worth. Build income that grows.

**Body:**
Three times a week, you get a 10-minute finance micro-lesson. Not theory. Actionable blueprints you can execute the same day.

**You'll learn:**
- How to find your first side-income client (before you even have a portfolio)
- The salary negotiation script that works — with templates
- How to build an emergency fund without deprivation
- Investing basics for people who've never invested
- The wealth mindset shift that changes your income ceiling

**The promise:** In 30 days, you'll have made more progress on your financial goals than in the last 3 years.

2. **Simple format:** "What changed? What didn't? Would you recommend us?"
3. **Incentive:** $10 credit or 1 month free for featured testimonials

### Validation
- Include full name, age, tier, and months/months for authenticity
- All testimonials must be verified (we can contact the person)
- Use real metrics where possible ("$7,000 raise," "8 lbs")
