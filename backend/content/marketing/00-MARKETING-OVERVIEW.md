# Marketing Content — Overview

## Audience
Burnt-out adults aged 35-55 who feel underpaid, over-extended, and directionless. They crave structure but don't have time to build it themselves.

## Channels

| Channel | Purpose | File |
|---------|---------|------|
| Landing Page | Primary conversion | `01-landing-page.md` |
| Email Welcome Sequence | New subscriber nurture (3 emails) | `02-email-welcome-sequence.md` |
| Social Media | Brand awareness, engagement | `03-social-posts.md` |
| Paid Ad Copy | Acquisition | `04-paid-ad-copy.md` |

## Brand Voice Guidelines
- **Warm but direct** — like a coach who genuinely cares but won't let you make excuses
- **No fluff** — mid-lifers have zero patience for wellness jargon
- **Age-aware** — acknowledges the 35-55 reality without making it sound like a disease
- **Action-oriented** — every piece of copy points to one clear action
- **Empathetic** — they're tired, stressed, and skeptical. Meet them where they are.
