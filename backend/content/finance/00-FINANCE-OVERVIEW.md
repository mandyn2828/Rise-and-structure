# Finance Micro-Lessons — Overview

## Philosophy
Mid-lifers feel underpaid and over-extended. They want to earn more but lack the roadmap. These micro-lessons bridge that gap — not with get-rich-quick schemes, but with practical, actionable systems to increase income, build security, and create options.

## Weekly Rotation (3 lessons/week)
| Day | Theme | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| Mon | Income Building | Side-hustles, negotiation, career growth |
| Wed | Money Management | Budgeting, debt, savings, investing basics |
| Fri | Wealth Mindset | Beliefs, risk, long-term thinking |

## Content Format per Lesson
1. **The Principle** — one clear, memorable concept
2. **The Action** — what to do today (15-30 min max)
3. **The Resource** — one tool, book, or reference

## Difficulty Levels
- **Starter:** Never saved, in debt, needs basics
- **Builder:** Has some savings, wants to earn more
- **Thriver:** Financially stable, wants to optimize

## File Naming
- `{day_number}-{week_slug}-{topic-slug}.md`
- Day 1 = Monday (Income), Day 3 = Wednesday (Money Management), Day 5 = Friday (Wealth Mindset)
