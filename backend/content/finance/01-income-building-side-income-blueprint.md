# Day 1 — Income Building: The Side-Income Starter Blueprint

## The Principle
Your earning potential expands in three directions: **raise** (earn more at your job), **side** (earn on the side), **asset** (money that works for you). Most people only focus on one. Start with the side.

## The Action: Your Side-Income Audit (15 min)

### Step 1: Inventory Your Assets
List everything you can monetize:

| Asset Category | Examples | Do You Have This? |
|---------------|----------|-------------------|
| Skills | Writing, coding, design, speaking, organizing, coaching, teaching | |
| Knowledge | Industry expertise, a hobby you've mastered, a problem you've solved | |
| Networks | LinkedIn connections, industry contacts, community groups | |
| Tools | Camera, car, extra room, laptop, software | |
| Time | Evenings, weekends, 30-min pockets | |

### Step 2: Pick Your Path

| Path | Starter | Builder | Thriver |
|------|---------|---------|---------|
| Freelancing | Upwork/Fiverr (your existing skill) | Premium clients via referrals | Agency/model |
| Digital Products | Sell one template/guide | Build a mini-course | Course ecosystem |
| Coaching | Free sessions for testimonials | Paid 1:1 coaching | Group programs |
| Content | Niche social presence | Lead magnet → email list | Paid newsletter |
| Service | Walk dogs, clean, assemble furniture | Hire others to do it | Service business |

### Step 3: Your One Action This Week
Pick ONE path. Do ONE thing:
- Create a profile on Upwork/Fiverr (30 min)
- Or: Write down 5 people who could be your first freelance client
- Or: Outline one digital product you could create in a weekend

## Starter Goal
List 3 skills you already have that someone would pay for. (You have more than you think.)

## Thriver Goal
Connect with 3 people in your network who could refer you premium side-work. Send them a genuine, specific message.

## Resource
**Book:** *The Side Hustle Bible* by Anyta — or free alternative: listen to "Side Hustle School" podcast (10-min episodes).

## Tracking Prompt
> "What's one skill I undervalue that others would pay for?"

## Coach's Note
Don't let perfect be the enemy of started. A $500/month side income that takes 5 hours/week is a 300% hourly return compared to minimum wage. Start small. Start now.
