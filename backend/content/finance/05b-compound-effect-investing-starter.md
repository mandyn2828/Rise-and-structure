# Day 5 (Week B) — Wealth Mindset: The Compound Effect

## The Principle
Wealth is not built in big moments. It's built in small, consistent actions that compound over time. A $5 daily coffee invested instead spent = $120,000 in 30 years at 7%. Small edges, time, and patience beat luck every time.

## The Action: Start Your Investment Engine (20 min)

### Step 1: Know the Vehicles (5 min)

| Vehicle | Use For | Risk Level |
|---------|---------|------------|
| High-yield savings | Emergency fund (3-6 months expenses) | Very low |
| 401(k) / 403(b) | Retirement, especially if employer matches | Medium-Long |
| Roth IRA | Tax-free retirement growth | Medium-Long |
| Index Funds (VOO, VTI) | Long-term wealth building | Medium |
| Individual stocks | Speculation (high risk, small portion only) | High |
| CDs / Bonds | Short-term savings (1-5 year horizon) | Low |

### Step 2: The Order of Operations (5 min)

Do these IN ORDER:

1. ✅ **Emergency fund** (3-6 months expenses)
2. ✅ **Employer 401(k) match** (free money — always take it)
3. ✅ **High-interest debt** (credit card > 7% interest — pay this off before investing)
4. ✅ **Roth IRA** (up to $7,000/year for 2025)
5. ✅ **Additional 401(k) contributions** (beyond match)
6. ✅ **Taxable brokerage account** (index funds)

Where are you on this list? Write your current step: ______

### Step 3: One Action Today (10 min)

**Pick ONE:**
- If you have a 401(k) at work, log in. Is it set up? Are you getting the full match?
- Open a Roth IRA at Vanguard, Fidelity, or Schwab (free, takes 10 min)
- Buy ONE share of VOO (S&P 500 index fund) — around $500
- Or set up recurring investment of $50/month

## Starter Goal
Find out if your employer offers a 401(k) match. If yes, make sure you're contributing at least enough to get the full match. Free money.

## Builder Goal
Open a Roth IRA. Fund it with $50-100/month.

## Thriver Goal
Max out your Roth IRA ($7k/year = ~$583/month). Set up automatic monthly contributions.
