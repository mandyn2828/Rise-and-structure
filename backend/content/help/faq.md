# Rise & Structure — In-App FAQ

---

## What is Rise & Structure?

Rise & Structure is a daily guidance system for mid-lifers (ages 35-55) who want to rebuild their health, their income, and their sense of direction.

Every day, at your chosen time, you get:
- A **wellness routine** (5-15 minutes — mobility, nutrition, sleep, stress)
- A **mindset coaching prompt** (3-5 minutes — reflection, practice, affirmation)
- On Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, a **finance micro-lesson** (5-10 minutes — income skills, money management, wealth mindset)

No decisions. No planning. Just open the app and follow your daily guidance.

---

## How does the daily plan work?

When you sign up, you complete a quick assessment (5 steps, about 3 minutes total). Based on your answers, we build a personalized plan that includes:

- Your **tier** (Starter, Builder, or Thriver)
- Your **primary focus** (health, income, direction, or all three)
- Your **delivery time** (morning, lunch, or evening)
- Your **first week of content** (selected from the library based on your needs)

Every day at your chosen time, your content arrives. You do it, check it off, and move on. The next day, new content arrives automatically. Content rotates every two weeks so you never get bored.

## What do the tiers mean? (Starter / Builder / Thriver)

### 🌱 Starter
**Best if:** You're new to wellness and finance habits. You want structure but need it super simple.

- Daily routines: 5-10 minutes
- Focus on foundational habits, one small win at a time
- Low pressure — build consistency first

### 🏃 Builder
**Best if:** You have some healthy habits but need more structure and higher-level challenges.

- Daily routines: 10-20 minutes
- Focus on building on what works, leveling up steadily
- Guided progress with measurable growth

### 🚀 Thriver
**Best if:** You're consistent but want depth, optimization, and advanced practices.

- Daily routines: 15-30 minutes
- Focus on optimization, mental discipline, income acceleration
- High standards with compound growth

You can change your tier anytime in **Settings > My Plan**. Your content adjusts automatically.

## How do I track my progress?

Your **Dashboard** shows everything:

- **Current streak** — how many consecutive days you've completed
- **Consistency percentage** — your completion rate over the last 30 days
- **Wellness score trend** — updated monthly based on your check-ins
- **Badges earned** — milestones you've hit along the way

You can also go to **Library > Past Content** to review or repeat any previous day's routines.

---

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Settings > Account > Cancel Subscription**. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Your data is saved for 90 days. If you come back, everything will be right where you left it.

If you're within your 7-day free trial, cancel anytime — no charge.

---

## Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes. All courses are available both in the app and as downloadable PDFs.

If you purchase a course, you own it forever. You can access it from **Library > My Courses** on any device. Subscribers get 15% off all course purchases.
