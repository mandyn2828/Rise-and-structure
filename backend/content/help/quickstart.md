# Rise & Structure — Quickstart Guide

**Welcome. Here's how to hit the ground running.**

---

## Step 1: Complete Your Assessment

When you first sign up, you'll go through a 5-step assessment. It takes about 3 minutes.

**What you'll be asked:**
1. **What brings you here?** — Health, income, direction, or all three
2. **Wellness baseline** — Your current energy, activity, sleep, nutrition, and stress levels (1-5 scale)
3. **Mindset check-in** — Your mental state, emotional challenges, and what you want to feel more of
4. **Finance goals** — Your current situation, frustrations, and income target
5. **Preferences** — Time commitment and delivery time

**Be honest.** This isn't a test. The more honest you are, the better your personalized plan will be.

## Step 2: Review Your Personalized Plan

After your assessment, you'll see your **Personalized Plan Reveal**. This screen shows:

- **Your Wellness Score** (out of 5)

- **Your primary focus** — What we'll emphasize most based on your goals
- **Your daily content mix** — What each day will look like
- **Your first week preview** — What's coming up Day 1 through Day 7
- **Your 30-day goal** — What you told us you want to achieve

**Take a screenshot of this page.** It's your roadmap for the coming month.

## Step 3: Your First Day — What to Expect

At your chosen delivery time on your first day, you'll receive:

### 🏋️ Wellness Routine (5-15 min)
- A structured movement or practice session
- Includes instructions, modifications, and a tracking prompt
- Just follow along — don't judge, don't perfect, just do

### 🧠 Mindset Prompt (3-5 min)
- A morning (or afternoon/evening) reflection question
- Practice exercise you can do in 2 minutes
- Affirmation to carry with you
- Evening check-in question

### 💰 Finance Lesson (Mon/Wed/Fri only, 5-10 min)
- A practical, actionable micro-lesson
- Includes one specific action to take today
- A tracking prompt for accountability

**To complete your day:** Do the wellness routine, read (and optionally journal on) the mindset prompt, and check the box. That's it.

---

## Step 4: Building Streaks and Habits

Consistency is more important than intensity. Here's how to build it:

### The First Week
- **Days 1-3 feel awkward.** Your brain and body are building new pathways. This is normal.
- **Days 4-5 feel easier.** You start to look forward to your routine.
- **Days 6-7 feel like momentum.** You don't want to break the streak.

### If You Miss a Day
- **One missed day:** No problem. Just do tomorrow's routine.
- **Two missed days:** Do a 5-minute version to restart.
- **A whole week missed:** You haven't failed. You paused. Start again — Day 1 is waiting.

### The 5-Minute Rule
On days when you really don't want to do it, commit to just 5 minutes. That's it. Most of the time, 5 minutes turns into 10 or 15. But even if it doesn't, 5 minutes counts as a completed day.
