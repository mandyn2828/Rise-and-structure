# Step 2: Wellness Baseline

**Screen: "How's your body feeling?"**

---

## Headline
> Let's check your wellness starting point.

## Subheadline
> Be honest — this isn't a test. We need a baseline so we can track your progress.

---

## Question 1: Energy Levels (last 2 weeks)

*On a scale of 1-5, how would you describe your energy throughout most days?*

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Exhausted all day, dragging through everything |
| 2 | Low energy, need caffeine to function, crash by 3pm |
| 3 | Okay — some good hours, some slumps |
| 4 | Generally good energy, occasional dips |
| 5 | Consistently energized throughout the day |

---

## Question 2: Physical Activity

*On a scale of 1-5, how would you describe your current movement habits?*

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Sedentary — I sit 10+ hours a day and don't exercise |
| 2 | Minimal — I move when I have to (chores, errands) |
| 3 | Light — I walk sometimes but no structured exercise |
| 4 | Moderate — I exercise 1-2x/week |
| 5 | Active — I exercise 3+ times/week consistently |

---

## Question 3: Sleep Quality

*On a scale of 1-5, how would you describe your sleep?*

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Terrible — can't fall asleep, wake up often, <5 hours |
| 2 | Poor — take long to fall asleep, wake up tired |
| 3 | Fair — 6-7 hours but not restorative |
| 4 | Good — 7+ hours, wake up feeling rested most days |
| 5 | Excellent — consistent 7-9 hours, deep sleep |

---

## Question 4: Nutrition

*On a scale of 1-5, how would you describe your eating habits?*

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Mostly processed food, irregular meals, high sugar |
| 2 | Some home cooking but lots of takeout/snacking |
| 3 | Mixed — some good habits, some not |
| 4 | Generally healthy, mostly home-cooked |
| 5 | Well-balanced, intentional eating most of the time |

---

## Question 5: Stress Management

*On a scale of 1-5, how well do you manage stress?*

| Rating | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 1 | Overwhelmed — stress controls my life |
| 2 | Stressed — I know I need help but don't have tools |
| 3 | Managing — I cope but not in healthy ways |
| 4 | Good — I have practices that help |
| 5 | Excellent — I handle stress well and recover quickly |

---

## On Completion
Calculate a **Wellness Score** (average of 5 areas, 1-5 scale)

Store: `{energy_level, activity_level, sleep_quality, nutrition_level, stress_management, wellness_baseline_score}`
