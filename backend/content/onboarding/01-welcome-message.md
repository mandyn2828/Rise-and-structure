## Welcome Message

Hi {{first_name}},

Welcome to Rise & Structure.

You signed up because something in your life needs to change. Maybe it's your health — the extra weight, the poor sleep, the "I can't keep up" energy. Maybe it's your income — feeling underpaid, stuck, or ready for something more. Maybe it's the sense that you've lost direction somewhere between 35 and 55.

We get it. We built this for you.

**Here's how this works:**

Every day, you'll get:
- A **wellness micro-routine** (5-15 minutes — mobility, nutrition, sleep, stress)
- A **mindset prompt** (a reflection and a practice, not fluff)
- On **Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays**, a finance micro-lesson (income skills, money management, wealth mindset)

All of it is designed for someone with limited time and zero patience for wellness BS. No 90-minute yoga classes. No "just manifest more money." Just structured, automated guidance that actually works.

**Your first step:**

In the next 3 minutes, complete your **Getting Started Checklist** inside the app. This sets your tier (Starter, Builder, or Thriver) and your goals. Once that's done, your Day 1 content will unlock.

You've got this.

— The Rise & Structure Team
