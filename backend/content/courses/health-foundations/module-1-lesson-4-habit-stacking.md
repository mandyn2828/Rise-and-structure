## The Habit Stack Formula

The best way to build a new habit is to attach it to something you already do:

> **"After [current habit], I will [new habit]."**

Your morning already has anchors:

| Anchor Habit | Stack This |
|-------------|-----------|
| Waking up | Drink 500ml water |
| Brushing teeth | Do 2 min neck stretches |
| Showering | End with 15s cold rinse |
| Coffee brewing | Do 5 min mobility |

**Today's action:** Write 3 habit stacks for your morning. Use the formula above.

---

## The Identity Shift

Habits stick when they become part of who you think you are.

| Goal | Identity |
|------|---------|
| Drink water | "I'm the type of person who hydrates first." |
| Exercise | "I'm someone who moves daily." |
| Meditate | "I'm someone who starts calmly." |

You don't need to believe it yet. You just need to act as if. The belief follows the action.
