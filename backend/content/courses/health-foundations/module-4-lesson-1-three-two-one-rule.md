## What You'll Learn
The simplest sleep protocol ever invented — three numbers that fix 80% of sleep problems.

---

## The Rule

```
3 hours before bed: No more large meals
2 hours before bed: No more work
1 hour before bed: No more screens
```

That's it. Three simple boundaries.

---

## Why It Works

### 3 Hours — No More Large Meals
Digestion requires energy. If you're digesting while trying to sleep, your body is working instead of resting. Large meals before bed also increase the likelihood of acid reflux and blood sugar disruptions that wake you up at 3am.

**You can still have a small snack** (handful of almonds, banana, warm milk) if you're hungry.

### 2 Hours — No More Work
Your brain needs a transition from "problem-solving mode" to "rest mode." If you check email at 9pm, your brain is still processing at 11pm.

**Instead:** Read fiction, listen to music, have a conversation, take a bath.

### 1 Hour — No More Screens
Blue light suppresses melatonin production by up to 50%. Your phone, laptop, and TV all emit blue light.

**Instead:** Read a physical book, journal, stretch, meditate, talk to someone.

---

## The Exception

If you absolutely must use screens in the hour before bed:
- Use night mode (blue light filter)
- Dim the brightness to minimum
- Keep the screen at arm's length

But honestly: just put the phone down.
