# The Morning Reset — Lesson 2: The 10-Minute Mobility Sequence

**Module 1 of 4 | Day 2 | Time: 12 min**

---

## What You'll Learn
A 10-minute morning mobility routine you can do anywhere — no equipment, no space required.

---

## Why Mobility First?

Your body stiffens while you sleep. Fluid accumulates in joints. Muscles shorten. If you jump straight into movement or (worse) sit at a desk for 8 hours, you're asking for aches and injuries.

This sequence wakes up your joints, lubricates your connective tissue, and signals your nervous system that it's safe to move.

---

## The Sequence

Do each move slowly. No bouncing. Breath is the guide.

### 1. Cat-Cow (2 min)
- On hands and knees
- Inhale: drop belly, lift chest, look up
- Exhale: round spine, tuck chin
- 10 slow breaths

### 2. Hip Circles (1 min)
- Standing, hands on hips
- 30 seconds clockwise, 30 seconds counter
- Make the circles bigger each rotation

### 3. Thoracic Twist (2 min)
- Sit cross-legged or on a chair
- Twist upper body to the right, hand on opposite knee
- Breathe deep into the twist
- Hold 6 breaths each side

### 4. Forward Fold (2 min)
- Standing, fold at hips
- Bend knees generously if hamstrings are tight
- Let your head hang. Shake your head "yes" and "no"
- Hold 8 breaths

### 5. Side Stretch (1 min)
- Reach right arm overhead, lean left
- Hold 30 seconds. Switch.

### 6. Deep Breaths (2 min)
- Stand tall. Hands on ribs.
- Inhale for 4 counts — feel ribs expand sideways
- Exhale for 6 counts — feel ribs contract
- Repeat 10 cycles

---

## Pro Tips
- If you can't get on the floor, do this standing
- Morning stiffness is normal — it means your body rested deeply
- This is not a workout. Don't push into pain. The goal is waking up, not limbering up.
