## The Plate Formula

Every meal should look like this on your plate:

```
┌─────────────────────┐
│                     │
│    🥗 VEGGIES       │
│    2 fist-sized     │
│     portions        │
│                     │
├──────────┬──────────┤
│ 🥩 PROTEIN│ 🍚 CARBS │
│ Palm-sized│ 1 cupped │
│ portion   │  hand    │
├──────────┴──────────┤
│ 🥑 HEALTHY FAT      │
│ Thumb-sized portion │
└─────────────────────┘
```

### No Counting. Just Use Your Hand.

| Food Group | Portion | Your Hand |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Protein | Palm size | Your palm (minus fingers) |
| Vegetables | 2 fist sizes | Two of your fists |
| Complex carbs | 1 cupped hand | Your hand cupped |
| Healthy fats | 1 thumb | Your thumb |

---

## Why This Works

- **Protein** keeps you full and maintains muscle
- **Veggies** fill you up with fiber and micronutrients
- **Complex carbs** (quinoa, sweet potato, brown rice) give you steady energy
- **Healthy fats** (avocado, olive oil, nuts) support hormones and brain function

If a meal doesn't have at least 3 of these 4 components, it's going to leave you hungry.

---

## One Meal Upgrade

Don't change everything. Upgrade one meal.

**The rule:** Pick breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Apply the Plate Formula. Do it for 3 days.

**Example breakfast upgrade:**
- ❌ Before: Coffee + toast
- ✅ After: 2 eggs (protein) + spinach (veggies) + 1/2 avocado (fat) + side of berries (carbs)
