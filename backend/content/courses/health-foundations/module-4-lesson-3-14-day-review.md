## Build Your Health Operating Manual

Take 10 minutes and write a one-page document answering:

### My Minimum Viable Routine (non-negotiable)
> *"Every single day, I will __________"*

Keep it to 3 things max. This is your floor — the minimum you do on your worst day.

### My Weekly Non-Negotiables
> *"Every week, I will __________"*

- How many walks?
- How many mobility sessions?
- How many nights of good sleep?

### My Early Warning Signs
> *"When I notice __________, I know I'm slipping and need to recommit."*

This is crucial. Catch yourself before you fall off completely.

### My Reboot Plan
> *"If I miss 3+ days, I will __________ to restart."*

Not "start over from scratch." Restart. Ease back in.
