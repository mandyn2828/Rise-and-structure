## Course Structure (per module)
1. **Lesson** (800-1500 words + real-world example)
2. **Action** (one concrete task)
3. **Tracking prompt** (for journaling)
4. **Resource** (tool, book, template)

- Side-Income Starter: higher price (perceived value higher — income upside)
