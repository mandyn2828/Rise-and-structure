## The Content Engine

| Frequency | Content | Platform |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Daily | 1 tip or insight (2-3 sentences) | LinkedIn / Twitter |
| 2x/week | 1 longer post or thread | LinkedIn / Twitter |
| Weekly | 1 email (if you have a list) | Email |
| Monthly | 1 case study or result | Website / LinkedIn |

**Total: 2-3 hours/week** for consistent visibility.

---

## The 3-Bucket Content Strategy

### Bucket 1: Educational (60%)
- Tips, how-tos, frameworks
- "3 things nobody tells you about [topic]"
- "The [X] framework for [Y]"

### Bucket 2: Relatable (25%)
- Struggles, lessons, behind the scenes
- "I failed at [X] for 6 months. Here's what I learned."
- "Honest update on my [project] journey"

### Bucket 3: Promotional (15%)
- "I have capacity for 2 new clients"
- "New product launch"
- "Case study: how I helped [client] achieve [result]"

---

## The Lead Magnet

Create one free thing that attracts your ideal client:

| If you sell... | Lead magnet |
|---------------|------------|
| Freelance writing | "5 email templates that get client replies" |
| Coaching | "The 3-session coaching framework PDF" |
| Digital products | "Free budget tracker template" |

**Gate it behind an email signup.** Now you have a list.
