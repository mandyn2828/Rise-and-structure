## What You'll Learn
How to scale your freelancing from your first project to a consistent $1,000+/month.

---

## The $100 → $1,000 Path

### Phase 1: First $100 (Days 1-14)
- 1-2 small projects
- Low rates ($25-50)
- Building portfolio and confidence
- **Goal:** Prove it's possible

### Phase 2: First $500 (Days 14-30)
- 3-5 projects
- Moderate rates ($50-75)
- First repeat clients
- **Goal:** Build consistency

### Phase 3: $1,000+/month (Days 30-60)
- 5-10 projects
- Higher rates ($75-150)
- Repeat clients + referrals
- **Goal:** Build momentum

---

## The 80/20 Rule

Look at your clients and projects. You'll likely find that:

- 20% of your clients generate 80% of your revenue
- 20% of your services generate 80% of your income

**Double down on the 20%.** Drop the low-value services. Focus on what pays best and what you enjoy most.

---

## The Retainer Model

Instead of one-off projects, offer retainers:

| One-Off Project | Monthly Retainer |
|----------------|------------------|
| $500, one time | $400/month for 6 months |
| You find new work constantly | Steady, predictable income |
| Onboarding every time | Deeper relationship |
| Higher admin overhead | Lower admin overhead |

**How to offer it:** "Would you prefer a one-time project or a monthly retainer where I handle [service] on an ongoing basis?"
