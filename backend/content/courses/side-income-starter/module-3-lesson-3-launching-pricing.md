## Pricing Psychology

| Price | Perception | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| $5-7 | Impulse buy | Printables, single templates |
| $12-17 | Value buy | Trackers, guides, checklists |
| $27-37 | Serious investment | Mini-courses, bundles |
| $47-97 | Premium | Full courses, coaching packages |

**Rule of thumb:** 50-100x your target hourly rate for templates/guides. If you want to earn $50/hr, price your product at $15-27.

---

## The Launch Sequence

### Pre-Launch (3 days before)
- Share what you're building on social media
- "I'm working on something — who struggles with [problem]?"
- Build anticipation, not sales

### Launch Day
- Post: "I made a thing! [Product name] is live. It helps [audience] [solve problem]."
- Send to your email list (if you have one)
- Share in relevant communities (LinkedIn groups, Reddit, Facebook)

### Post-Launch (Week 1)
- Share early customer feedback
- "Didn't expect this response — 10 people have already used [product]"
- Offer a limited-time discount (first 50 buyers get 20% off)

## The First 10 Sales

Your goal is not 1,000 sales. It's 10 sales.

| Sale # | How to Get It |
|--------|--------------|
| 1-3 | Close friends who support you |
| 4-6 | People in your network who need it |
| 7-10 | Cold audience (social media, community) |

Once you have 10 sales, you have proof. You have testimonials. You have momentum.
