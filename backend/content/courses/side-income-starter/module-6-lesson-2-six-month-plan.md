## The 6-Month Roadmap

### Month 1: Foundation
- Choose your path (freelance, product, or coaching)
- Create your service menu / product outline
- Get your first client or sale
- **Goal: $100**

### Month 2: Build
- Raise rates (if freelancing)
- Create first digital product (if product path)
- Get 2-3 repeat clients
- **Goal: $500/month**

### Month 3: Momentum
- Set up systems (invoicing, scheduling, content)
- Get first referral (without asking)
- Start building an email list
- **Goal: $500-1,000/month**

### Months 4-5: Scale
- Raise rates again
- Create product #2
- Consider a retainer client
- **Goal: $1,000-2,000/month**

### Month 6: Evaluate
- Review: what worked, what didn't
- Decide: is this a side income or a new career?
- Plan next 6 months
- **Goal: Sustainable $1,000-3,000/month**

---

## The Quit Threshold

When should you consider quitting your day job?

**Financial threshold:** Side income = 3x your monthly expenses
**Emotional threshold:** Your side income brings more joy/satisfaction than your job
**Practical threshold:** You have 6 months of savings as a buffer

If you hit all three, it's time to have the conversation.

---

## The 6-Month Investment

Time commitment by month:
- Month 1: 5-8 hours/week
- Month 2: 6-10 hours/week
- Month 3: 8-12 hours/week
- Month 4-6: 10-15 hours/week

That's roughly 1-2 hours per weekday. You can find it. You have the time audit to prove it.
