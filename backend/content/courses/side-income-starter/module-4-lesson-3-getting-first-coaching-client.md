# Coaching Path — Lesson 3: Getting Your First Coaching Client

**Module 4 of 6 | Day 14 | Time: 12 min"

---

## What You'll Learn
How to get your first 3 coaching clients — including a script that works.

---

## Where Your First Clients Come From

| Source | How | Difficulty |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Your existing network | "I'm offering free coaching to build my skills — interested?" | Easy |
| Social media | Post about your coaching offer | Medium |
| Past clients (other services) | "Would you like to go deeper?" | Easy |
| Communities (LinkedIn groups, Reddit, Facebook) | Offer value first, then mention coaching | Medium |

---

## The Offer Script

Here's a message that works. Use it exactly or adapt:

> *"Hey [Name],*
>
> *I'm building my coaching practice and looking for 3 people to coach for free (or heavily discounted) in exchange for honest feedback.*
>
> *I specialize in helping [niche] achieve [outcome].*
>
> *Would you be open to a 30-minute discovery call to see if this is a fit?"*

---

## The Discovery Call Script

| Phase | You Say | Time |
|-------|---------|------|
| Opening | "What's been going on? What brought you here?" | 5 min |
| Exploration | "What have you tried? What's the real challenge?" | 10 min |
| Possibility | "What would it look like if this were solved?" | 5 min |
| Offer | "Here's how I help people get there. Interested?" | 5 min |
| Close | "Great. Our first session is [day/time]. Here's what to prepare." | 5 min |

---

## The Conversion Rate

Expect:
- Out of 10 people you message → 5 reply
- Out of 5 discovery calls → 2-3 book coaching
- Out of 3 coaching clients → 1-2 will refer others

**Don't get discouraged by "no." It's a numbers game.**
