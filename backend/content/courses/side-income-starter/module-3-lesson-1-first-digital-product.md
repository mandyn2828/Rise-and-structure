## The Weekend Product Sprint

**You can create a digital product in one weekend:**

| Day | Task | Time |
|-----|------|------|
| Friday night | Choose idea, outline content | 1 hour |
| Saturday AM | Write/create the content | 3 hours |
| Saturday PM | Design/layout | 2 hours |
| Sunday AM | Set up sales page (Gumroad) | 1 hour |
| Sunday PM | Launch to 10 friends for feedback | 1 hour |

**Total: 8 hours from idea to launch.**

---

## Validation Before Creation

Here's a secret: you can sell a product before you create it.

1. Outline the product (what it is, what problem it solves)
2. Set up a simple sales page (Gumroad, Payhip)
3. Share with 10 people who might need it
4. If 3+ people offer to buy → create it
5. If nobody bites → choose a different idea
