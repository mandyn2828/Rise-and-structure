# Mindset & Audit — Lesson 3: Your Service Menu

**Module 1 of 6 | Day 3 | Time: 15 min**

---

## What You'll Learn
How to package your existing skills into 3 clear services people can buy.

---

## The Problem

Most people say "I can do anything" or "I'm flexible." That's a mistake. Clients don't buy "I can help you." They buy specific outcomes.

---

## The 3-Service Rule

Pick 3 services you'll offer. No more. No less.

### Step 1: Inventory Your Skills
List everything you're good at:

- From your day job (project management? writing? spreadsheets? design?)
- From your hobbies (photography? cooking? fitness? organizing?)
- From your life experience (parenting? budgeting? moving? renovating?)
- From your education (degrees, certifications, training)

### Step 2: Pick 3 That Pay
Which of those skills solve a problem someone would pay for?

| If you're good at... | People will pay you to... |
|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Writing | Write emails, blogs, social posts |
| Spreadsheets | Build budgets, models, trackers |
| Organizing | Declutter their home or digital files |
| Presenting | Coach them on public speaking |
| Designing | Create logos, social graphics, presentations |
| Teaching | Tutor their kid or teach them a skill |

### Step 3: Package Them
Give each service a name and a price.

**Example — Freelance Writer:**
1. Blog writing — $200/post (1,500 words, researched, SEO)
2. Email newsletter — $500/month (4 emails, weekly)
3. Social content — $300/week (5 posts, 2 platforms)

**Example — Virtual Assistant:**
1. Inbox management — $300/month (clear inbox daily)
2. Calendar scheduling — $200/month (manage appointments)
3. Research & reporting — $400/month (market research)

---

## Pricing Rule

**Start lower than you want, raise fast.**

| Month | Rate | Why |
|-------|------|-----|
| 1-2 | $X | Building portfolio, getting testimonials |
| 3-4 | $X * 1.5 | You have proof, demand is growing |
| 5+ | $X * 2-3 | You're established, selective |
