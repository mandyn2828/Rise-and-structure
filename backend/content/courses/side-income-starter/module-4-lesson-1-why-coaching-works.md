## The Coaching Market

People pay for transformation. Not information.

| Information | Transformation |
|------------|---------------|
| A book about budgeting | A system that saves you $500/month |
| A blog about health | 30 days of coaching that changes your habits |
| A guide to career growth | Weekly calls that help you navigate your next step |

**You don't need to be an expert. You need to be slightly ahead of someone else.**

---

## Your Coaching Niche

The best coaching niche answers three questions:

1. **Who?** (Tired dads? Career-changers? Freelancers? Overwhelmed managers?)
2. **What problem?** (Can't stick to habits? Stuck in a job? Can't say no?)
3. **What outcome?** (More energy? A promotion? A side income?)

**Example niches:**
- "I help mid-career professionals start a side income in 90 days."
- "I help burnt-out dads rebuild their health without quitting their job."
- "I help recent immigrants navigate the U.S. job market."

---

## The 5-Client Strategy

Your first 5 clients should be free or heavily discounted.

| Client | Price | Goal |
|--------|-------|------|
| 1 | Free | Practice your framework |
| 2 | Free | Refine your approach |
| 3 | Free | Get a testimonial |
| 4 | $50 | Test willingness to pay |
| 5 | $100 | Build confidence |

**After 5 clients:** Raise your rate to $200-500/session.

---

## The Discovery Call

Before anyone buys coaching, they need a discovery call. Here's the structure:

1. **Their problem** — "What's going on? What brings you here?"
2. **Their goal** — "What would success look like in 90 days?"
3. **Their obstacle** — "What's stopping you from getting there alone?"
4. **Your offer** — "Here's how I help people [achieve X]. Interested?"
