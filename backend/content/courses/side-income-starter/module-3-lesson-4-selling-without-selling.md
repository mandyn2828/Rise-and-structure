## What You'll Learn
How to attract buyers without feeling like a salesperson.

---

## The Problem

Most people hate selling because it feels like:

- Pushing something on people who don't want it
- Exaggerating to make a sale
- Being annoying in DMs

**Good news:** You don't have to sell that way. You can sell by being helpful.

---

## Content-Based Selling

Instead of "Buy my product," share content that helps people:

| Instead of This | Do This |
|----------------|---------|
| "Buy my budget tracker" | "3 simple budget categories that changed my finances" |
| "Buy my Notion template" | "How I organize my week in 10 minutes" |
| "Buy my course" | "The #1 mistake people make starting a side income" |
| "Buy my ebook" | "5 things I wish I knew before I started freelancing" |

The pattern: **Give value first. Mention your product naturally at the end.**

---

## The 80/20 Content Rule

80% of what you share: Free value (tips, insights, stories)
20%: "Here's how to go deeper (my product)"

If you have 1,000 followers, and you post 10x:
- 8 posts: Free value
- 2 posts: Product mention

## Platforms That Work for Digital Products

| Platform | Best For | Strategy |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Gumroad discover | Templates, guides | SEO-optimized listing |
| Etsy | Printables, planners | Search-driven |
| LinkedIn | Professional products | Share expertise daily |
| Twitter/X | Quick tips, building in public | Threads |
| Pinterest | Visual products | Pin your product images |
