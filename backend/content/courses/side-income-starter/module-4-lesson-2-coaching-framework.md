# Coaching Path — Lesson 2: The Coaching Framework

**Module 4 of 6 | Day 13 | Time: 12 min**

## What You'll Learn
A simple 3-session framework you can use with every client.

---

## The 3-Session Package

Don't do single sessions. Offer a package.

| Session | Theme | What Happens |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Diagnosis & Goal | Understand their situation, set clear outcomes |
| 2 | Action Plan | Create a step-by-step plan, assign actions |
| 3 | Accountability & Review | Check progress, adjust, plan next steps |

**Price for 3 sessions:** $150-500 depending on your experience.

---

## Session 1: Diagnosis & Goal (60 min)

**Questions to ask:**
1. "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"
2. "What have you tried so far?"
3. "What's worked? What hasn't?"
4. "What would success look like in 90 days?"
5. "What's the cost of NOT solving this?"

**Output:** A written summary of their situation + agreed-upon goal.

---

## Session 2: Action Plan (45 min)

**Structure:**
1. Review their homework (observations from the week)
2. Identify patterns ("I notice that when X happens, you Y")
3. Create 3 specific actions for the next week
4. Assign accountability (daily check-in, progress log, etc.)

**Output:** A written action plan with specific, time-bound steps.

---

## Session 3: Accountability & Review (45 min)

**Structure:**
1. What worked? (celebrate wins)
2. What didn't? (no judgment — just learning)
3. What needs to change? (adjust the plan)
4. Next steps (continue with coaching, graduate to self-guided, or move to a different focus)

**Output:** Progress assessment + next phase plan.

---

## The Golden Rule of Coaching

**Don't give advice. Ask questions.**

Instead of "You should exercise more," say:
> "What's one thing you could do this week that would move the needle on your energy?"

People buy into answers they discover themselves.
