## What You'll Learn
The three types of side income — and which one to start with based on your energy and goals.

---

## The Spectrum

### Level 1: Active Income (Trading Time for Money)

| Example | How It Works | $/Hour |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Freelancing (your skill) | You do the work, you get paid | $25-150+ |
| Service work (walking dogs, cleaning) | You do the task, you get paid | $20-50 |
| Consulting/coaching | You advise, you get paid | $50-500+ |

**Pros:** Fastest to start, highest immediate return
**Cons:** You earn only when you work (no passive)

### Level 2: Productized Income (Create Once, Sell Many Times)

| Example | How It Works | $/Hour (long-term) |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Digital products (templates, guides) | Create once, sell forever | $500+/hour |
| Online courses | Create once, sell forever | $500+/hour |
| Printables / Notion templates | Create once, sell forever | $500+/hour |

**Pros:** Scalable, semi-passive
**Cons:** Takes longer to create, requires upfront work

### Level 3: Asset Income (Money That Works)

| Example | How It Works |
|---------|-------------|
| Investments | Money grows without your time |
| Rental properties | Real estate generates cash flow |
| Royalties | IP generates ongoing payments |

**Pros:** Truly passive
**Cons:** Requires capital, slow to build

---

## Where to Start

**If you have 5 hours/week and need money fast:** Start with Level 1 (freelancing).

**If you have 10+ hours/week and want long-term scalability:** Start with Level 2 (digital products).

**If you have capital and patience:** Add Level 3 (investing) alongside 1 or 2.

---

## The Starter Strategy

Month 1-2: **Freelance** your existing skill (fast cash, builds confidence)
Month 3-4: **Create one digital product** (your knowledge in a template/guide)
Month 5-6: **Scale** what works (raise rates, build systems)

This course covers all three paths.
