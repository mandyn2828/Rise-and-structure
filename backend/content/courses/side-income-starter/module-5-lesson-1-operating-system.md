## The 2-Hour Rule

Most people think they need 20 hours/week for a side income. They don't. They need 2 focused hours.

| 2 Distracted Hours | 2 Focused Hours |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| Phone notifications on | Phone on silent, in another room |
| Email open in background | Single task, full attention |
| TV on | Music or quiet |
| Multiple tabs open | One document/tool open |
| Frequent interruptions | 45-min block, no interruptions |

2 focused hours > 8 distracted hours. Every time.

---

## Time Blocking

Block time on your calendar. Non-negotiable. Treat it like a client meeting.

| Day | Time | Block Type |
|-----|------|-----------|
| Monday | 7-8pm | Client work (projects) |
| Wednesday | 7-8pm | Marketing (content, outreach) |
| Saturday | 9-11am | Deep work (product creation, strategy) |

**Total: 4 hours/week.** That's enough to build $500-1,000/month.
