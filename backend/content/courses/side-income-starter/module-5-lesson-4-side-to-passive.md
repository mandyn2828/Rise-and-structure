# Systems & Automation — Lesson 4: From Side Income to Passive Income

**Module 5 of 6 | Day 18 | Time: 10 min"

---

## What You'll Learn
How to shift from active side income (trading time) to semi-passive income (assets that work).

---

## The Transition Path

```
Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Active Income
Freelancing, coaching, service work
→ $500-1,500/month (trading time)

Phase 2 (Months 3-6): Productize
Turn your knowledge into digital products
→ $200-500/month (semi-passive)

Phase 3 (Months 6-12): Scale
Systems + team + automation
→ $1,000-3,000/month (hybrid)
```

---

## The Asset-Building Mindset

Every time you do client work, ask: **"How can I turn this into something I only create once?"**

| Active Work | Asset Version |
|------------|---------------|
| Custom budget for one client | Budget template for 100+ buyers |
| Coaching one person | Group coaching program |
| Writing one email for a client | Email course for many buyers |
| Designing one presentation | Presentation template |

---

## Automations to Set Up

| Automation | Tool | What It Does |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Invoice reminders | Wave/Stripe | Sends reminders automatically |
| Email follow-ups | Mailchimp (free) | Sequences for leads |
| Calendar booking | Calendly | No back-and-forth scheduling |
| Social scheduling | Buffer (free) | Schedule posts in advance |
| Payment collection | Stripe/Gumroad | Auto-collects payments |

---

## The 1% Rule

Every week, spend 1 hour improving your systems. By the end of the year, that's 52 hours of improvements — and your business runs without you.
