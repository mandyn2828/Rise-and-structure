## Course Description
> Willpower is not a personality trait — it's a skill you can train. This course gives you the mental tools to stay focused, resist distractions, follow through on commitments, and stop quitting when things get hard.

## Module 1: The Willpower Myth (Days 1-3)

### Lesson 1: What Discipline Actually Is
- The problem with "just try harder"
- Willpower as a finite resource (ego depletion)
- The real secret: discipline is environment + systems + identity
- **Action:** Identify ONE area where you keep trying willpower and failing
- **Resource:** Ego depletion worksheet

### Lesson 2: The Identity Shift
- "I'm not a disciplined person" is a story, not a fact
- How to adopt identities that make discipline automatic
- The 2-word identity exercise
- **Action:** Write your new identity statement: "I'm someone who ______"
- **Example:** "I'm someone who follows through on what I say I'll do."

### Lesson 3: Environment Design
- Why your environment beats your willpower every time
- Friction reduction: make good habits easy, bad habits hard
- The 30-second rule (if starting a good habit takes >30s, streamline it)
- **Action:** Change ONE thing in your environment to make a good habit easier
- **Tracking:** Were there fewer obstacles to doing the right thing today?

## Module 2: Focus & Attention (Days 4-7)

### Lesson 4: The Attention Crisis
- Multitasking is a myth: your brain can focus on ONE thing at a time
- The cost of context-switching (up to 40% productivity loss)
- Your attention is your most valuable asset
- **Action:** Track how many times you switch tasks in one hour
- **Resource:** Focus audit template

### Lesson 5: Deep Work
- What deep work is and why it's rare
- The 45-minute deep work block (phone away, browser closed, one task)
- How to schedule deep work when you're busy
- **Action:** Schedule one 45-min deep work block tomorrow
- **Tracking:** How many minutes of focused work did I do today?

### Lesson 6: The Dopamine Detox
- Why you can't focus: your brain is addicted to stimulation
- Notifications, social media, and the dopamine loop
- The 24-hour social media pause
- **Action:** Take ONE social media app off your phone for 24 hours
- **Tracking:** What did I notice when I wasn't reaching for my phone?

### Lesson 7: Single-Tasking
- The Pomodoro technique (25 min work, 5 min break)
- Mono-tasking vs. multi-tasking
- The "one page, one task" rule
- **Action:** Practice Pomodoro for 3 cycles today

## Module 3: Overcoming Resistance (Days 8-10)

### Lesson 8: What Resistance Looks Like
- Procrastination, perfectionism, "I'll do it later"
- Excuses are protective stories: "I'm too tired," "I don't have time"
- Naming resistance strips it of power
- **Action:** Write down the top 3 ways you resist doing hard things

### Lesson 9: The 5-Second Rule
- When you have an impulse to act on a goal, you have 5 seconds to act
- If you don't move within 5 seconds, your brain talks you out of it
- Count backward: 5-4-3-2-1-GO and physically move
- **Action:** Practice the 5-second rule every time you hesitate today

### Lesson 10: The "Do It Badly" Permission
- Perfectionism is the #1 killer of action
- Give yourself permission to do good enough
- "Done is better than perfect"
- **Action:** Do one thing today "badly" — just get it done

## Module 4: Resilience & Grit (Days 11-13)

### Lesson 11: The Dip
- Every worthwhile endeavor has a "dip" — the hard middle
- Most people quit in the dip
- How to recognize the dip vs. when to actually quit
- **Action:** Identify where you currently are: start, dip, or plateau?

### Lesson 12: The Stoic Pause
- Between stimulus and response there is a space
- In that space is your power to choose
- The 10-second rule: count to 10 before reacting to anything
- **Action:** Practice the 10-second pause before 3 decisions today

### Lesson 13: Resilience as a Muscle
- Mental toughness is built, not born
- The discomfort habit: do one hard thing every day
- Cold showers, early mornings, hard conversations
- **Action:** Do ONE thing today that makes you slightly uncomfortable (on purpose)

## Module 5: Sustainable Discipline (Day 14)

### Lesson 14: Your Operating System
- Discipline is not about being hard on yourself forever
- Systems beat willpower: building routines that automate good decisions
- The 4 R's: Reduce friction, Re-design environment, Re-frame identity, Reinforce wins
- The monthly review practice
- **Action:** Write your personal Discipline Operating Manual (1 page)
