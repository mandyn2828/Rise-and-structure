## Course Description
> The minimum effective dose of health for busy mid-lifers. No gym membership required. No meal plans. Just four foundational habits that change everything.

## Module 1: The Morning Reset (Days 1-4)

### Lesson 1: Why Morning Matters
- Why how you start your morning predicts your whole day
- The cortisol awakening response — how to harness it
- The 10-minute morning routine (mobility + hydration + breath)
- **Action:** Commit to a 10-min morning routine for the next 4 days
- **Resource:** Free guided morning audio (5 min)

### Lesson 2: Hydration as Medicine
- Most fatigue is dehydration
- The 2L formula: how to actually drink it
- Morning water before caffeine: non-negotiable
- **Action:** Start every day with 500ml water before coffee/tea
- **Tracking:** Did I hit 2L today? Yes/No

### Lesson 3: Morning Mobility
- Why mobility is the #1 anti-aging practice
- The 5-min daily sequence (seated or standing)
- Consistency over intensity
- **Action:** Follow the 5-min mobility video
- **Tracking:** Flexibility rating (1-10) before and after

### Lesson 4: Stack & Celebrate
- How habit stacking works (attach new habit to existing one)
- The identity reframe: "I am someone who starts their day intentionally"
- Celebrate small wins to reinforce the habit
- **Action:** Create your morning stack formula
- **Review:** How did 4 days feel? What changed?

## Module 2: Fuel Without Drama (Days 5-8)

### Lesson 5: The Protein Priority
- Why mid-lifers need 0.7g protein per lb of body weight
- The protein cliff: you need more than you think
- Simple ways to add protein without meal prepping for hours
- **Action:** Calculate your protein target. Hit it for 3 days.

### Lesson 6: The Sugar Tamer
- The 3pm crash is not inevitable
- How to spot hidden sugar (bread, sauces, "healthy" snacks)
- The 3-day sugar reset
- **Action:** Replace one daily sugar source with a protein alternative

### Lesson 7: Eating Without Rules
- Intuitive eating for the mid-life body
- The difference between hunger, boredom, and stress eating
- The pause practice: 10-second pause before eating
- **Action:** Practice the pause at every meal today

### Lesson 8: One Meal Upgrade
- Don't change everything — upgrade one meal
- The Plate Formula: Protein + Veggies + Complex Carb + Fat
- How to make one meal "bulletproof"
- **Action:** Upgrade your lunch (or dinner) using the Plate Formula
- **Review:** Which upgrade was easiest? Hardest?

## Module 3: Stress to Strength (Days 9-11)

### Lesson 9: The Stress Spectrum
- Good stress (eustress) vs. bad stress (distress)
- How chronic stress makes you sick, tired, and fat
- The stress audit: what's actually stressing you?
- **Action:** Complete the stress audit worksheet
- **Resource:** Stress audit PDF template

### Lesson 10: The Breath Reset
- 60 seconds to lower your heart rate
- Box breathing (4-4-4-4) — the Navy SEAL method
- When to use it: before meetings, when angry, can't sleep
- **Action:** Practice box breathing 3x today

### Lesson 11: Movement as Stress Relief
- Why intense exercise can make stress worse
- Zone 2 cardio: walking, cycling easy, swimming
- The 20-min rule: move for 20 min to clear cortisol
- **Action:** Take a 20-min walk with no phone

## Module 4: Sleep for Results (Days 12-14)

### Lesson 12: Sleep Is a Skill
- The 7-hour minimum — non-negotiable
- Why you wake up at 3am (and what to do about it)
- The 30-min wind-down protocol
- **Action:** Create your wind-down ritual. Follow it 3 nights.

### Lesson 13: The Sleep Environment
- Light, temperature, noise, and comfort
- The $100 sleep upgrade (blackout curtains, white noise, cooling pillow)
- Screen curfew: 1 hour before bed
- **Action:** Remove phone from bedroom tonight

### Lesson 14: The 14-Day Review
- What worked, what didn't, what to continue
- How to build your own health system from these modules
- Creating your "minimum viable routine" going forward
- **Action:** Write your 1-page health operating manual
- **Certificate:** Printable completion certificate
