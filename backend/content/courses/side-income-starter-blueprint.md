# Side-Income Starter — Course Blueprint

**Slug:** side-income-starter
**Price:** $49
**Duration:** 21 days
**Modules:** 6

## Course Description
> Stop trading time for money at your day job and start building income streams that actually grow. This is not about "passive income" fantasies — it's about practical, proven side income paths that work for busy adults with full-time jobs.

## Module 1: The Side-Income Mindset (Days 1-3)

### Lesson 1: Why You Need a Side Income
- The single-income trap: why it's dangerous
- How $500/month in side income changes your life (debt freedom, options, peace of mind)
- The myth of "I don't have time" — time audit exercise
- **Action:** Complete time audit (find 5 hours/week you didn't know you had)
- **Resource:** Time audit worksheet PDF

### Lesson 2: The Side-Income Spectrum
- Low-effort vs. high-effort side incomes
- Active (trading time for money) vs. passive (creating assets once, selling many times)
- Where you should start based on your current energy level
- **Action:** Identify where you are on the spectrum. Choose a starting path.
- **Tracking:** How much time can I realistically commit per week?

### Lesson 3: The First $100
- Why $100 is the most important milestone
- The 7-day sprint: launch something imperfect in 7 days
- Case study: How someone in your situation made their first $100
- **Action:** Set a $100 goal and a 7-day deadline

## Module 2: The Freelancer Path (Days 4-8)

### Lesson 4: Your Service Menu
- How to package your existing skills into services people buy
- The 3-service rule: offer 3 specific services, not "I can do anything"
- Pricing: how to charge what you're worth
- **Action:** Write your 3-service menu with prices
- **Resource:** Service menu template PDF

### Lesson 5: Finding Your First 3 Clients
- Where your first clients are (existing network > cold outreach)
- The referral script: how to ask without feeling awkward
- Platforms: Upwork, Contra, LinkedIn Services Marketplace
- **Action:** Reach out to 5 people in your network. Use the referral script.

### Lesson 6: Delivering & Getting Paid
- How to over-deliver without over-working
- Contracts, invoices, and payment terms (simple templates)
- Getting testimonials and referrals after every project
- **Action:** Send your first invoice (even for a test project)

### Lesson 7: From $100 to $1,000
- Raising your rates: when and how
- The 80/20 rule: double down on what works
- Building recurring clients (retainers)
- **Action:** Identify one client you could convert to a retainer

### Lesson 8: Case Study Deep Dive
- 3 real case studies of people who built freelancing into $1k+/month
- What they did, what they learned, what they'd do differently
- Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
- **Action:** Pick ONE case study strategy to implement this week

## Module 3: The Digital Product Path (Days 9-13)

### Lesson 9: Your First Digital Product
- What sells: templates, guides, checklists, mini-courses
- How to create a product in one weekend
- Validation before creation: sell it before you build it
- **Action:** Brainstorm 3 product ideas. Choose one.

### Lesson 10: Creating Your Product
- Tools: Canva, Notion, Gumroad, Teachable
- The minimum viable product mindset
- Design tips for non-designers
- **Action:** Create the outline/framework of your product

### Lesson 11: Pricing & Launching
- Pricing psychology: $7, $17, $27, $47 — which is right for you?
- Launch strategies: email list, social media, partnerships
- The launch checklist
- **Action:** Set up your product page on Gumroad or similar

### Lesson 12: Selling Without Selling
- Content that sells: how to attract buyers without feeling "salesy"
- Building an email list from day one
- Social proof: reviews, testimonials, social shares
- **Action:** Write 3 social posts about your product

### Lesson 13: Product Launch Week
- Daily checklist for launch week
- Handling objections and questions
- Post-launch analysis: what worked, what didn't
- **Action:** Launch your product. Celebrate.

## Module 4: The Coaching/Consulting Path (Days 14-17)

### Lesson 14: Why Coaching Works for Mid-Lifers
- Your life experience is an asset
- The coaching market: people pay for transformation
- Coaching vs. consulting: what's the difference?
- **Action:** Define your coaching niche (who you help, with what problem)

### Lesson 15: The Transformation Framework
- From problem → solution: framing your offer
- The 3-session package vs. the ongoing retainer
- Pricing: $50-$500/session based on experience
- **Action:** Outline your coaching offer (problem, solution, price, outcomes)

### Lesson 16: Getting Your First Coaching Client
- Free coaching for testimonials (the 5-client strategy)
- How to run a discovery call
- Case study: from free to $200/session in 30 days
- **Action:** Offer free coaching to 3 people today

### Lesson 17: Scaling: Group Coaching
- 1:1 coaching → small group (3-5 people)
- Group pricing: 3x the value for 2x the price
- How to run a group program
- **Action:** Outline your first group program

## Module 5: Systems & Automation (Days 18-19)

### Lesson 18: Your Side-Income Operating System
- Calendar blocking for side income work
- Tools stack: what you actually need (notion + stripe + calendar)
- Time management: the 2-hour rule (2 focused hours > 8 distracted hours)
- **Action:** Set up your weekly side-income schedule

### Lesson 19: From Side Income to Passive Income
- The asset-building mindset
- How to create once and sell many times
- Affiliates, partnerships, and content that works while you sleep
- **Action:** Identify one "evergreen" asset you can create

## Module 6: The Future (Days 20-21)

### Lesson 20: When to Quit Your Day Job
- The financial safety threshold (3x your side income = your salary)
- The emotional readiness check
- Transitioning without burning bridges
- **Action:** Calculate your "quit number"

### Lesson 21: Your 6-Month Side-Income Plan
- Goals by month
- Key milestones
- Accountability system
- **Action:** Write your 6-month plan. Share it with one accountability partner.
