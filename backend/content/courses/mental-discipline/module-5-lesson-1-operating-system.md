## The Four R's

| R | Question | Example |
|---|----------|---------|
| Reduce | "What friction can I remove?" | Lay out clothes, prep meals |
| Re-design | "What in my environment needs to change?" | Move phone, clear workspace |
| Re-frame | "What identity shift would make this automatic?" | "I'm someone who exercises" |
| Reinforce | "How do I celebrate and track progress?" | Streak, checkmark, reward |

---

## Your Discipline Operating Manual

Create a one-page document with these sections:

### Non-Negotiables (What I do every day, no excuses)

1. _______________________________________
2. _______________________________________
3. _______________________________________

Keep it to 3 items max. These are your floor on your worst day.

### Environment Rules (How I set up my space)

1. Phone is _______ (not in bedroom / face down / in another room)
2. Morning routine is _______ (water, move, intention)
3. Work space is _______ (clean, dedicated, phone-free)

### Emergency Procedures (What I do when I'm slipping)

1. If I miss 1 day: Don't panic. Just do the routine tomorrow.
2. If I miss 3 days: Reset. Do one 5-minute version.
3. If I miss 7 days: Re-read this operating manual. Restart.

### My Identity Statement

> *"I am someone who __________."*

## The Monthly Review

Every month, answer:

1. What worked this month?
2. What didn't?
3. What needs to change?
4. What's my ONE focus for next month?
