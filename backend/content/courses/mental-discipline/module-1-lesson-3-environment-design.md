# Willpower Myth — Lesson 3: Environment Design

**Module 1 of 5 | Day 3 | Time: 10 min**

---

## What You'll Learn
How to redesign your environment so good habits are easy and bad habits are hard.

---

## The 30-Second Rule

Every action takes a certain amount of startup energy:

| Action | Startup Energy |
|--------|---------------|
| Eat a cookie on the counter | 0 seconds |
| Eat a cookie in the high cabinet | 3 seconds |
| Eat a cookie in the garage | 30 seconds |
| Eat a cookie in the store (must go buy) | 30+ minutes |

**Rule:** Make good habits take ≤30 seconds to start. Make bad habits take ≥30 seconds to start.

---

## Environment Audit

Walk through your home and identify friction points:

### Good Habits — Reduce Friction
- Workout clothes laid out the night before? (30s → 0s)
- Water bottle on your desk? (10 steps → arm's reach)
- Running shoes by the door? (2 min search → grab and go)

### Bad Habits — Increase Friction
- Phone on silent, face down, across the room
- TV remote in a drawer (not on the coffee table)
- Snacks in a hard-to-reach cabinet
- Social media apps off your home screen

---

## The 1% Rule

Improve your environment by 1% every day.

**This week:**
- Monday: Move phone charger out of bedroom
- Tuesday: Put workout clothes out tonight
- Wednesday: Clear kitchen counter of snacks
- Thursday: Put water bottle on desk
- Friday: Delete social media apps from phone (use browser only)
- Saturday: Set up a dedicated workspace
- Sunday: Audit the week — what worked?
