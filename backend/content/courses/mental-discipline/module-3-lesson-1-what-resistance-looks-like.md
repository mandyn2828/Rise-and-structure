## Resistance

Author Steven Pressfield calls it **Resistance** (capital R). It's the force that acts against human creativity, growth, and progress.

It shows up as:

| Symptom | Resistance Voice |
|---------|----------------|
| Procrastination | "I'll do it later. It's not that urgent." |
| Perfectionism | "I'm not ready yet. I need to prepare more." |
| Self-doubt | "Who am I to do this? Someone else is better." |
| Busyness | "I'm too busy with [less important things]." |
| Distraction | Let me just check [phone/email/social] first." |
| Exhaustion | "I'm too tired. I'll do it tomorrow." |

---

## The Resistance Pattern

Resistance follows a predictable pattern:

1. You decide to do something important
2. Resistance appears (within 24 hours)
3. You feel the urge to avoid, delay, or distract
4. You either push through or give in

**The key insight:** Resistance is strongest RIGHT BEFORE the breakthrough. If you can push through the first 10 minutes, it usually dissolves.
