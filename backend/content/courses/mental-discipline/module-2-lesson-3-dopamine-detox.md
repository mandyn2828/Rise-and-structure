# Focus & Attention — Lesson 3: The Dopamine Detox

**Module 2 of 5 | Day 6 | Time: 12 min**

---

## What You'll Learn
Why you can't focus — and what happens when you go 24 hours without cheap dopamine.

---

## The Problem

Your phone, social media, and streaming services are designed to deliver small hits of dopamine constantly:

- Notification → dopamine
- New like → dopamine
- New post → dopamine
- New email → dopamine

Your brain adapts. It needs more stimulation to get the same hit. So you scroll faster, check more, and eventually:

- Reading a book feels boring
- Sitting in silence feels unbearable
- Working on one task for 30 minutes feels impossible

---

## The 24-Hour Dopamine Detox

**For 24 hours, eliminate:**
- Social media (all platforms)
- News apps
- YouTube (except for learning, not entertainment)
- Video games
- Streaming (Netflix, etc.)
- Pornography

**Allowed:**
- Work (if it's your job)
- Reading (physical books)
- Exercise
- Conversations (in person or phone)
- Nature
- Sleep

---

## What Happens

**First 4 hours:** Intense urge to check phone. Feels unbearable. This is withdrawal.

**4-8 hours:** Boredom sets in. You'll realize how often you reach for your phone.

**8-16 hours:** Your mind starts to quiet. You might have new ideas or insights.

**16-24 hours:** You feel calmer. More present. Less anxious. Things feel slower.

---

## Your Turn

**Action:** Pick a 24-hour window this week for a dopamine detox. No phone scrolling. No social media. No streaming. See how it feels.

**Journal prompt:** *"What did I reach for when I was bored? What happened when I sat with the boredom instead?"*
