## Getting Started

### What is Rise & Structure?
A daily guidance system for mid-lifers (ages 35-55) who want to rebuild their health, income, and direction. Each day, you get a wellness routine, a mindset coaching prompt, and (3x/week) a finance micro-lesson. Everything is personalized to your starting point and delivered at your chosen time.

### How is this different from other wellness apps?
Most apps assume you're already motivated and just need tracking. Rise & Structure handles the **thinking** for you. You don't plan. You don't decide what to do. You just open the app at your scheduled time and follow your daily guidance. No decisions. No guilt. Just showing up.

### How much time do I need per day?
- **Starter tier:** 5-10 minutes
- **Builder tier:** 10-20 minutes
- **Thriver tier:** 15-30 minutes

You can change tiers anytime in Settings.

### When do I get my daily content?
You choose your delivery time during onboarding:
- 🌅 Morning (6-9am) — start your day with intention
- 🌤️ Lunch (12-2pm) — midday reset
- 🌆 Evening (7-10pm) — wind down and reflect

You can change this anytime in Settings.

### Do I need a gym or equipment?
No. All wellness routines use bodyweight movements and everyday household items. A wall, a chair, and your own body are all you need.

## Your Account

### How do I change my tier?
Go to **Settings > My Plan > Tier**. You can switch from Starter to Builder to Thriver (or back down) anytime. Your content will adjust automatically.

### How do I change my delivery time?
Go to **Settings > Notifications > Delivery Time**. Changes take effect the next day.

### I want to reset my progress. Can I start over?
Yes. Go to **Settings > My Plan > Reset Journey**. This clears your streak and starts Day 1 again. Your tier and preferences are preserved.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to **Settings > Account > Cancel Subscription**. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. Your data is saved for 90 days in case you return.

## Content & Routines

### What kind of wellness routines are there?
Content rotates on a 2-week cycle:
- **Week A (Foundation):** Morning mobility, nutrition basics, stress shedding, sleep hygiene, energy audit, active recovery
- **Week B (Build):** Strength foundations, meal prep, breathwork, sleep optimization, movement snacks, outdoor adventure

Every routine has Starter/Builder/Thriver variations.

### I have a bad back / bad knees / other limitations. Can I still do this?
Yes. Every routine includes modifications. If something ever hurts (not just discomfort), stop and try the modified version. If you have a medical condition, check with your doctor before starting any movement program.

### I'm not good at writing/journaling. Do I have to?
No. The mindset prompts include optional journaling, but you can also just read and reflect silently. Some people write one sentence. Some just think about the question. Do what works for you.

### I already know basic finance. Will this be too beginner?
The finance content tiers automatically based on your assessment. If you selected "experienced" on your onboarding, you'll get more advanced content. You can also skip any lesson and the system will adjust.

### Can I go back and redo old content?
Yes. Go to **Library > Past Content** to browse or repeat any previous day's routines, prompts, or lessons.

## Progress & Streaks

### What counts as a "completed day"?
You complete a day when you've opened and finished the daily wellness routine AND the daily mindset prompt. Finance lessons (3x/week) are bonus, not required for streak completion.

### What happens if I miss a day?
Nothing bad. Your streak resets, but your progress is preserved. The app will send a gentle re-engagement notification. There's no punishment, no guilt, no "you lost your progress" messaging. Just start again tomorrow.

### Can I see my progress over time?
Yes. Go to **Dashboard > Progress**. You'll see:
- Current streak
- Consistency percentage (last 30 days)
- Wellness score trend (updated monthly)
- Badges earned

### What are badges?
Badges are earned for milestones:

| Badge | Requirement |
|-------|------------|
| 🔥 Foundation Streak | 7-day streak |
| 🔥 Momentum Streak | 14-day streak |
| 🔥 Unstoppable Streak | 30-day streak |
| 💪 Wellness Warrior | 15 wellness routines completed |
| 🧠 Mindset Master | 15 mindset sessions completed |
| 💰 Income Builder | 10 finance lessons completed |
| 🏆 Complete Rebuild | All 3 pillars completed in one month |
