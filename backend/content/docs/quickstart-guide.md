## Welcome. Here's what to expect.

You've signed up. You've done your assessment. Now it's time to actually start.

## Day 0: Right Now

### What you've already done
✅ Picked your tier (Starter, Builder, or Thriver)
✅ Set your delivery time
✅ Told us your goals

### What to do right now
1. **Turn on notifications** — so you don't miss your daily content
2. **Set up your space** — find a spot where you can move, sit, and breathe without interruption for 5-15 minutes
3. **Tell one person** — accountability helps. Tell a friend or partner you're doing this.

## Days 1-7: Your First Week

### What you'll get every day

| Time | What Arrives | How Long |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Your chosen time | 🏋️ Wellness routine | 5-15 min |
| Your chosen time | 🧠 Mindset prompt | 3-5 min |
| Mon/Wed/Fri | 💰 Finance lesson | 5-10 min |

### Your job
1. Open the app (or check your email if you opted in)
2. Follow the wellness routine (just move, don't judge)
3. Read the mindset prompt (sit with it for 2 minutes)
4. Check the box ✅

**That's it.** You don't need to plan tomorrow. Tomorrow will arrive on its own.

### Pro tips for the first week
- **Don't skip two days in a row.** Missing one day is fine. Missing two makes it harder to come back.
- **Don't try to be perfect.** A 5-minute routine you actually do beats a 30-minute routine you avoid.
- **Your energy might drop before it rises.** This is normal. Your body is adapting. Stick with it.
- **Notice small wins.** Felt slightly more energy? That counts. Went to bed 15 minutes earlier? That counts.

## Your Tier Guide

### 🌱 Starter
Your focus is **consistency**. You're building the habit of showing up.
- Do the base routine only (no add-ons)
- If you miss a day, just do Day 1 again
- Goal: 5 minutes, every day

### 🏃 Builder
Your focus is **leveling up**. You're building on a foundation.
- Add the Builder extension to each routine
- Push yourself gently on the finance lessons (try one action each time)
- Goal: 10-15 minutes, 6 out of 7 days

### 🚀 Thriver
Your focus is **depth**. You're optimizing what already works.
- Do the full routine including Thriver extensions
- Apply insights from mindset to your day, immediately
- Goal: 15-30 minutes, daily

## What Not to Worry About

| Don't Worry About | Why |
|------------------|-----|
| Doing it perfectly | The goal is showing up, not getting an A+ |
| Comparing to others | Your journey is yours. Different tier, different life. |
| "Catching up" | You can't fall behind. The content waits for you. |
| Losing your streak | Streaks are motivation, not punishment. Break it? Start again. |
| Understanding everything | Some concepts click on Day 3. Some on Day 30. Some never. Doesn't matter. |
