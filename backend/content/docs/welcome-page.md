## Tips for Success

1. **Start small.** A 5-minute routine you actually do beats a 30-minute one you avoid.
2. **Don't miss two days in a row.** Missing one day is okay. Two makes it harder to come back.
3. **Trust the process.** The first week feels awkward. That's normal. The compound effect starts in Week 2.
4. **You're not behind.** There's no catching up. Every day is a fresh start.
