# Day 6 — Connection (Lighter Weekend Edition)

## Morning Prompt
> "We don't heal in isolation. We heal in connection. Today, focus on the people who matter."

## Reflection (Write for 3-5 min)

1. Who in my life makes me feel truly seen and understood?
2. Who have I been neglecting lately? (One text, one call, one visit — that's all it takes.)
3. What's one relationship I've let drift that I'd like to rebuild?
4. How often do I tell people I appreciate them? When was the last time?
5. What's one thing I need from my relationships that I'm not asking for?

## Practice: The Connection Action

Pick ONE:
- **Text someone** "I was just thinking about you and wanted to say hi. Hope you're doing well."
- **Call a parent, sibling, or old friend** — no agenda, just catch up
- **Write a genuine compliment** to someone and deliver it
- **Ask someone** "How are you really doing?" — and actually listen

### The Connection to Self
Connection also means being honest with yourself about what you need. Answer:
> "What do I need from others right now that I'm not getting? How can I either ask for it or give it to myself?"

## Affirmation (Repeat 3x today)
> "I am worthy of love and connection. I can reach out. I can let people in."

## Evening Check-in (2 min)
> "How did I connect today — with others or with myself? How did it feel?"

## Coach's Note
Mid-life is often lonely. Friends scatter. Family obligations pile up. The career treadmill leaves little time for depth. But loneliness is a signal, not a sentence. One meaningful conversation can shift everything. Make the call.
