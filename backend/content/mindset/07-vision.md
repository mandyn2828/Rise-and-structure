# Day 7 — Vision (Lighter Weekend Edition)

## Morning Prompt
> "Your past does not determine your future. Every moment is a chance to choose a new direction."

## Reflection (Write for 5 min)

1. If I could design my ideal day 5 years from now, what would it look like? (Describe it in detail — where you wake up, what you do, who's around, how you feel.)
2. What's one thing I want to be true about my life that isn't true right now?
3. If fear were not a factor, what would I pursue?
4. What's one small step I can take THIS WEEK toward that vision?
5. What am I deeply grateful for in this moment?

## Practice: The Vision Statement

Write a 2-3 sentence vision for your life ONE YEAR from now. Include:
- How you want to feel
- One health goal
- One financial/professional goal
- One relationship goal

**Example:**
> "One year from now, I feel strong, calm, and in control of my time. I exercise 4x a week without dreading it. I've started a side income that brings in $500/month. My relationship with my partner is more intentional and less transactional."

## Weekly Closeout

Take 5 min. Reflect on this week's mindset work:
1. What was the most challenging mindset topic this week?
2. What was the most helpful?
3. What's one mindset shift I want to carry into next week?
4. Rate my overall mental state this week (1-10). Why?

## Affirmation (Repeat 3x today)
> "My future is not a fixed destination. I am building it, one choice at a time."

## Evening Check-in (2 min)
> "What's one thing I'm excited about for next week? What's one thing I'm nervous about? What's one thing I can control about it?"

## Coach's Note
Vision without action is a daydream. Action without vision is a treadmill. You need both. This week you've done the vision work. Next week, the structure kicks in to help you act. End this week knowing: you're not lost. You're just in the process of finding your way.
