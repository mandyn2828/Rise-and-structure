# Day 3 — Purpose Reconnect

## Morning Prompt
> "You don't need to find your 'one true purpose.' You need to find what matters right now, in this chapter of your life."

## Reflection (Write for 5 min)

1. When did I last feel truly alive and engaged? What was I doing?
2. What's one thing I do that makes time disappear (flow state)?
3. If money were not a concern, how would I spend my typical Wednesday?
4. What problem in the world (or in my community) bothers me most?
5. What's one thing I was passionate about as a child that I've let go?

**The Insight:** Big gaps (e.g., high meaning, low time) show where your life is out of alignment.

**Today's Action:** Pick one area with a significant gap. Do ONE thing today that increases time spent there by even 5 minutes.

## Affirmation (Repeat 3x today)
> "Purpose is not a destination. It's the direction I choose, moment by moment."

## Evening Check-in (2 min)
> "What gave me energy today? What drained it? What's one thing I can do more of tomorrow?"

## Coach's Note
Don't wait for a lightning bolt of purpose. Purpose is attention + intention. What you give your attention to grows. What you intend, you move toward. Start small. Start now.
